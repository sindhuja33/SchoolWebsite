SANTHIDAM BETHANIA MATRICULATION SCHOOL (SBMS)

Santhidam Bethania Matriculation School is one of the premier educational institutions in Kanyakumari District. Established in 2003 and managed by the Bethany Sisters, a religious community founded by the late Archbishop Venerable Mar Ivanios, the school is dedicated to holistic learning and moulding children into independent individuals.

Our school envisions a mission in which students take the lead in changing Indian society for the better. We help learners act locally and think globally, growing with the freedom to think, act, and behave with core virtues. The goal is a community that is responsible, respectful, understanding, and trustworthy.
