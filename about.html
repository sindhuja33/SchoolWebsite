About GVIS

Building Learners, Leaders, and Responsible Citizens

Established in Chennai, Green Valley International School is committed to delivering meaningful education in a safe, inclusive, and inspiring environment.

Our Mission

To nurture every learner's potential through high-quality education, innovation, and value-based development.

Our Vision

To be a leading school in India that prepares compassionate, confident, and future-ready global citizens.

Core Values

Integrity

We encourage honesty, accountability, and ethical decision-making.

Respect

We celebrate diversity and build an inclusive school culture.

Excellence

We set high standards and support continuous improvement for every learner.
