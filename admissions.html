Admissions 2026-27

Join the Green Valley Learning Community

Our admissions process is transparent, child-friendly, and focused on readiness rather than pressure.

Eligibility

  • Pre-Primary to Grade 12 admissions open.
  • Age criteria based on academic year guidelines.
  • Transfer certificates required for Grade 2 onwards.

Documents Required

  • Birth certificate copy
  • Previous report cards
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Address proof and ID details

Important Dates

  • Application starts: January 10
  • Interaction sessions: February onwards
  • Academic year begins: June

Admission Process

Step 1: Submit Application

Complete the enquiry form through our contact page or visit our campus admissions desk.

Step 2: Interaction

A student and parent interaction is scheduled to understand learning needs and expectations.

Step 3: Confirmation

Admission confirmation is shared after document verification and fee payment.

Step 4: Orientation

Families attend orientation to understand school systems, curriculum, and campus facilities.
