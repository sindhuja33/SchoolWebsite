FAQs

Admission Questions Answered

What is the admission age for KG?

Children aged 34 years are eligible for KG.

Do you offer transportation?

Yes, school buses cover major routes in Kanyakumari district.

Is there an entrance test?

Assessment is conducted for grades I and above to understand learning needs.

Can I submit the form online?

Yes, submit inquiries online and our team will guide you through the steps.

Are scholarships available?

Merit-based scholarships are offered for outstanding academic performance.

When does the academic year start?

The academic year begins in June as per State Board guidelines.
